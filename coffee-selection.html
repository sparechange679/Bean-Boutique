Understanding Coffee

Roast Levels

Light Roast: Retains most origin characteristics, bright acidity, lighter body.

Medium Roast: Balanced flavor, moderate acidity, fuller body.

Dark Roast: Bold, smoky flavors, low acidity, full body.

Storage Tips

Store coffee in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Avoid refrigeration as it can introduce moisture.

Buy whole beans and grind just before brewing for maximum freshness. Use within 2-3 weeks of opening.

Grinding Guide

Coarse: French Press, Cold Brew

Medium: Drip Coffee Makers

Fine: Espresso, Moka Pot

Extra Fine: Turkish Coffee
