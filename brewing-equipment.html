Brewing Methods Guide

Espresso

Grind: Fine

Water Temp: 195-205°F

Brew Time: 25-30 seconds

Ratio: 1:2 (18g coffee to 36g espresso)

Produces concentrated, full-flavored coffee with crema. Perfect base for milk drinks.

Pour-Over

Grind: Medium

Water Temp: 195-205°F

Brew Time: 3-4 minutes

Ratio: 1:16 (25g coffee to 400g water)

Clean, bright cup that highlights origin characteristics. Requires attention and technique.

French Press

Grind: Coarse

Water Temp: 195-205°F

Brew Time: 4 minutes

Ratio: 1:15 (30g coffee to 450g water)

Full-bodied, rich coffee with natural oils. Simple and forgiving brewing method.

Cold Brew

Grind: Coarse

Water Temp: Cold/Room Temp

Brew Time: 12-24 hours

Ratio: 1:8 (concentrate) or 1:16 (ready to drink)

Smooth, sweet, low-acid coffee. Makes a concentrate that can be diluted to taste.
